Introduction to STARS

STARS is a public chain based on the BPOS consensus mechanism. In the future, it will create a block-chain ecosystem covering "public chain wallet and Defi products and block-chain cross-border payment platform and diversified DAPP applications". STARS adopts the internal and external circulation mechanism of "Dual Token Dual Economic Model "and "Dual currency dual track two currencies and two tracks", combined with a unique incentive system, customizable smart contracts, and a decentralized distributed service architecture to finally create A distributed intelligent financial ecosystem with deep scalability and sustainable development.

Core Advantages

  • 1. Support millions of users

    STARS has the underlying technical architecture of the block-chain that can handle tens of millions of daily active users.

  • 2. Free to use

    STARS uses decentralized anonymity as its design principle, and builds a "private Internet" on distributed nodes for free use by all the public.

  • 3. Easy upgrade and fault repair

    STARS-based applications naturally need to be able to support software upgrades during functional iterations.

  • 4. Low latency

    The innovative BPOS consensus mechanism ensures that the STARS high-performance public chain can provide users with a high-speed experience.

  • 5. Serial performance

    The multi-chain parallel mechanism gives STARS powerful serial performance.

  • 6. Parallel performance

    Large-scale applications need to divide the workload between multiple CPU and computers, and STARS can achieve multi-terminal parallelism.
